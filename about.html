About

Notes that connect research and engineering

This is a personal blog for research ideas, modeling experiments, implementation notes, and reading records. The goal is not only to keep conclusions, but also to preserve the conditions, evidence, and reasoning behind each decision.

Writing standards

  • Experiment posts keep data, commands, settings, and observations together.
  • Code posts separate implementation intent from verification.
  • Paper posts distinguish claims, direct evidence, and interpretation.

Interests

I am interested in eye tracking, emotion analysis, multimodal modeling, reproducible experiments, and research software design.
