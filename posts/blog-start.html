First post: principles for this blog

2026.06.29 3 min

I want this blog to work less like a gallery of finished outcomes and more like a record of how decisions were made. A post can be short, but it should leave enough context to reproduce the same conditions later.

What to keep

Experiment and implementation posts should keep the data, key settings, command line, observed differences, and next checks in one place. Even a failed condition is useful evidence for the next decision.

What to avoid

I will avoid conclusions that are not tied to evidence. For papers in particular, summaries and interpretations should stay separate, and important claims should be kept with direct source text.

Default shape

  • Problem: why this was checked.
  • Conditions: which environment and data were used.
  • Result: what changed.
  • Decision: what should happen next.
